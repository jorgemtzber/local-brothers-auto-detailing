FAQ

Objection killers. Keep it simple and confident.

Do you come mobile?

Yes — we offer mobile service across The Woodlands and nearby areas. Drop-off is available by appointment.

Do you need water or power?

Usually not — but if a job needs it (ex: heavy extraction), we’ll confirm in advance so there are no surprises.

How long does it take?

Most details range from 1.5 to 6 hours depending on package + vehicle condition. We’ll confirm after booking.

What if my car is very dirty?

No problem — we’ll let you know if extra time or a deep-clean add-on is needed after photos/inspection.

Do you do pet hair?

Yes — pet hair removal is available as an add-on for embedded hair.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Please reschedule as early as possible. Same-day cancellations may require a small fee depending on time reserved.
