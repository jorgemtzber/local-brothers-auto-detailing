QUALITY STANDARD

What You Get

A consistent, professional finish — designed to look premium in photos and in person.

Do you come mobile?

Yes — we offer mobile service across The Woodlands and nearby areas. Drop-off is available by appointment.

Do you need water or power?

Usually not — but for certain services (like heavy extraction), we’ll confirm in advance.

How long does it take?

Most details range from 1.5 to 6 hours depending on package + condition. We confirm after booking.

What if my car is very dirty?

No problem — we’ll let you know if extra time/add-ons are needed after photos/inspection.

Do you do pet hair?

Yes — pet hair removal is available as an add-on for embedded hair.

Cancellation policy?

Please reschedule early. Same-day cancellations may require a small fee depending on time reserved.
